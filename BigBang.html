Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB)


One of the key pieces of evidence supporting The Big Bang theory is the discovery of the Cosmic Microwave
Background (CMB). This faint glow permeating the universe provides a snapshot of the early moments, offering

crucial insights into the conditions that prevailed just after The Big Bang.
