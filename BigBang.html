BIG BANG

Unlocking the Secrets of The Big Bang

The Big Bang, a cosmic explosion approximately 13.8 billion years ago
, birthed our universe.Unlocking its secrets involves delving into the
fundamental nature of space,< time, and matter. Scientists explore cosmic
microwave background radiation, particle physics, and astronomical
observations to decipher the origins and evolution of the cosmos, piecing together
the puzzle of our existence.
